About Me

The Guitarist's Journey

With over 5 years of experience on the guitar, I've dedicated my life to mastering the instrument and pushing creative boundaries. As the lead guitarist of Nyxxed Berries, I blend technical precision with raw emotional expression, creating music that resonates with audiences worldwide.

My musical journey began in a small garage with a dream and a battered guitar. Today, I perform on stages across the globe, collaborating with talented musicians and creating unforgettable live experiences. Every note tells a story, every performance is a connection with the audience.

Musical Influences

Influenced by legends like Jimi Hendrix, David Gilmour, and modern innovators, I draw inspiration from diverse genres—rock, blues, progressive metal, and electronic music. This fusion creates a unique sound that's distinctly mine.

The Band

Nyxxed Berries

Progressive Rock | Alternative Metal | Electronic Fusion

Nyxxed Berries is a four-piece band that combines explosive energy with intricate musicianship. Formed in 2025, we've been pushing the boundaries of modern rock music with our unique blend of progressive rock, alternative metal, and electronic elements.
